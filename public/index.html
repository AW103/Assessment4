Good Vibes Only

Sometimes, we just need a good rant.

Witholding negative emotions and thought can be harmful to our mental and emotional health. That being said, we don't always want to share things with the people around us. Type out your negative thoughts below. When you're done typing, click the release button. Your words will not be saved, simply released. Need a breather? Click the flower. Breathe in and out as it grows and shrinks.

Now, time for some balance.

Life is a mixture of positives and negatives. Outweighing the negatives with positives is not a natural mindset for everyone. It is often a skill that takes diligence, time, and patience. Let's start now. Type a list of positive things. I started you off with a few of mine, but feel free to delete them if you don't like them. It can be things in your life, experiences, or just dreams and wishes.
